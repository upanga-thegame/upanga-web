Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Upanga: The Soul-Blade handles information when you use our mobile game, website, or related services.

1. Information We Collect

At this stage, Upanga: The Soul-Blade does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

Depending on the final Android release, the game may collect limited technical or gameplay-related information, such as:

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Basic crash or error information
  • General gameplay progress or settings stored on the device
  • Anonymous analytics, if analytics are added in the future

If analytics, ads, cloud saves, leaderboards, or login features are added later, this Privacy Policy should be updated before release.

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected by the game may be used to:

  • Operate and improve the game
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Improve performance
  • Understand general gameplay behavior
  • Respond to support requests
  • Maintain game security and stability

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

If third-party services are added in the future, such as analytics, crash reporting, advertising, cloud saves, or app store services, limited information may be processed by those providers according to their own privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

The Android version of Upanga: The Soul-Blade may use third-party services in the future, such as:

  • Google Play services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud save or leaderboard services

This page should be updated with specific third-party service links before those services are included in the live app.

5. Children’s Privacy

Upanga: The Soul-Blade is not currently intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

If the game is later directed toward children or made available to children under applicable privacy laws, this Privacy Policy and the game’s data practices should be reviewed and updated to meet all applicable child privacy requirements.

6. Data Storage and Security

Game progress and settings may be stored locally on the user’s device.

We take reasonable steps to protect any information handled by the game or website, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. User Choices

Users may be able to:

  • Delete the game from their device
  • Clear local app data through Android settings
  • Contact us with privacy questions
  • Request deletion of information if any personal information is collected in the future

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Upanga: The Soul-Blade, contact us at: upangadev@zohomail.com

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
