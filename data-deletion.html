Data Deletion

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of data related to Upanga: The Soul-Blade.

1. Current Data Collection Status

At this stage, Upanga: The Soul-Blade does not offer user accounts, cloud saves, login features, online profiles, leaderboards, or account-based services.

Because there are currently no user accounts, there may be no server-side account data for us to delete.

Game progress or settings may be stored locally on your device. You can remove local game data by deleting the app or clearing the app’s storage through your Android device settings.

2. How to Clear Local App Data on Android

You can usually clear locally stored app data by using Android settings:

  • Open Android Settings
  • Go to Apps
  • Select Upanga: The Soul-Blade
  • Tap Storage
  • Tap Clear Storage or Clear Data

The exact steps may vary depending on your Android device and version.

3. Requesting Data Deletion

If you believe Upanga: The Soul-Blade has collected personal information about you, or if future versions of the game add account features, you may request deletion by contacting us at: upgangadev@gmx.com

Please include:

  • Your name, if applicable
  • The email address associated with your request, if applicable
  • A brief description of the data you want deleted
  • Any relevant details that can help us identify the data

Do not include passwords, payment information, government ID numbers, or other sensitive information in your request.

4. What Happens After a Request

After receiving a valid deletion request, we will review the request and take reasonable steps to delete applicable data, unless we are required or permitted to keep certain information for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or technical reasons.

If no account or server-side data exists for the user, we may respond that there is no account data available for deletion.

5. Third-Party Services

If future versions of Upanga: The Soul-Blade use third-party services such as Google Play services, analytics, crash reporting, advertising, cloud saves, or leaderboards, those services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

If applicable, this page will be updated with additional deletion instructions for those services.

6. Future Account Features

If Upanga: The Soul-Blade later adds account creation, cloud saves, online profiles, leaderboards, or similar account-based features, this page should be updated to explain:

  • How users can delete their account in the app
  • How users can request account deletion from the web
  • What data is deleted
  • What data may be retained
  • How long deletion requests may take

7. Contact Us

For data deletion questions or requests, contact: upgangadev@gmx.com

Important Note: This generic Data Deletion page should be reviewed and updated before publishing the game on Google Play, especially if accounts, cloud saves, analytics, ads, purchases, online services, or third-party SDKs are added.
