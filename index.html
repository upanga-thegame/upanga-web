Upanga: The Soul-Blade

A tactical turn-based RPG of shattered spirits, ancient magic, and skeletal armies.

Command four heroes across isometric battlefields, restore the broken Soul-Blade, and face Bokor the Corrupted.

Story

The Soul-Blade is shattered, and the spirits of the ancient heroes are trapped. Bokor, a powerful necromancer, raises skeletal armies to conquer the land. You must gather the scattered fragments of the Soul-Blade, awaken the heroes, and restore balance to the world.

Features

Turn-Based Tactical Combat

Engage in strategic battles on an 8x8 isometric grid. Every decision matters.

Four Hero Party

Control a party of four unique heroes, each with their own abilities and playstyle.

Retro Stone-and-Pixel Style

A lovingly crafted retro aesthetic that harks back to the golden age of tactical RPGs.

Skeleton Enemies & Boss Battles

Face a variety of skeletal enemies and challenging bosses, led by the necromancer Bokor.
