The Shattered Soul Blade

Upanga, the Soul Blade, is a mystical blade forged by ancestral spirits at the beginning of time. It has been shattered by the evil witchdoctor - Bokor the Corrupted and split into four fragments. The fragments are corrupting the land and dividing the once united Kingdoms of Azuma. You must gather the fragments, reforge the blade and unite the Kingdoms.

Features

Turn-Based Tactical Combat

Engage in strategic battles on an isometric grid. Every decision matters.

Four Hero Party

Control a party of four unique heroes, each with their own abilities and playstyle.

African Diaspora Fantasy Style

An aesthetic inspired by African mythical, spiritual & mystic traditions

Unique Enemies & Boss Battles

Face a variety of enemies and challenging bosses, led by the necromancer Bokor.

Throwback RPG Style

Experience a throwback RPG style game with simple but challenging mechanics.

Exciting Rich Story

A classic story of good vs evil. Compelling dialogue sequences, enjoyment for the entire family.
