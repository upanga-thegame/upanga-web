The Shattered Soul-Blade

The Soul-Blade, once a beacon of hope, has been shattered by the necromancer Bokor. Its essence has been split into four spirit-fragments, corrupting the land and its guardians. You must gather these fragments to reforge the blade and restore balance.

Features

Turn-Based Tactical Combat

Engage in strategic battles on an 8x8 isometric grid. Every decision matters.

Four Hero Party

Control a party of four unique heroes, each with their own abilities and playstyle.

Modern Tribal Fantasy Style

A cinematic aesthetic inspired by African tribal fantasy design, polished for a modern audience.

Skeleton Enemies & Boss Battles

Face a variety of skeletal enemies and challenging bosses, led by the necromancer Bokor.

The Four Heroes

Four heroes, bound by fate, are all that stand against Bokor's corruption.

Osa the Warrior

A stalwart defender whose duty is to protect the innocent.

Kishi the Shapeshifter

A mysterious being who can take on the forms of defeated foes.

Aziza the Scout

A nimble rogue who moves through shadows to strike at the heart of the enemy.

Nganga the Shaman

A powerful mystic who communes with spirits to heal allies and curse enemies.
